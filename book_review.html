My Favorite Book Genre

My favorite genre of books to read is by far fantasy. There are so many worlds that people build that are fascinating to get into. A lot of books do follow similar tropes, but what makes them entertaining is how unique the different worlds are. New takes on old stories are fascinating, different concepts with the same creatures is what keeps fantasy interesting.

Reading vs Cinema

I think reading will always have a place in the world, even though movies and tv shows are really taking over in entertainment. Books take very little material resources to write, so anyone can make a story. Movies and shows are expensive to make and they take up very little time compared to books.
When reading you have to imagine what the author is telling you. The author created the world, but your mind is what makes it real. In movies you see the directors image, there is no room for you to imagine anything, you just consume what is put in front of you.
